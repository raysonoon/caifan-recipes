Tomato Egg

Description

Tomato Egg is an easy dish to make, perfect for any meal.

Ingredients

Serving size: 5 people

Sauce

  • 2 tomatoes, chopped into rough chunks
  • 3 tbsp ketchup
  • 1 stalk spring onion

Eggs

  • 5 eggs
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp white pepper

Instructions

  1. Chop the spring onions.
  2. Cut the tomatoes in chunks.
  3. Whisk 5 eggs with 1 tsp salt and 1/2 tsp white pepper.
  4. Add oil and then half of the chopped spring onions, followed by the tomatoes.
  5. Stir fry for a minute then add in 3 tbsp ketchup. Give it a good stir.
  6. Add sugar, pour in 50ml water and simmer for 30 seconds. Dish out and set aside.
  7. In a clean non stick pan, heat up 2 tbsp oil on medium heat and pour in the egg mixture.
  8. Using a spatula, push the cooked egg from the sides to the centre until no visible liquid egg remain. You will achieve a ‘rippled’ scramble egg.
  9. Pour the cooked tomato mixture and mix it quickly with the scrambled egg.
  10. Sprinkle the remaining half of the chopped spring onions. Serve hot.
