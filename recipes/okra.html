Okra Stir-fry (Lady Finger)

Description

One of my favourite vegetables :)

Ingredients

Serving size: 4 people

  • 250g okra, sliced cross-wise
  • 1 tomato, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, finely chopped
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1 carrot, cubed
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 2 tsp light soy sauce
  • pinch of salt
  • dash of white pepper

Instructions

  1. Prepare aromatics and vegetables
  2. Pour vegetable oil and stir fry garlic and onions until golden brown
  3. Add carrots first and stir fry for about a minute. Then add okra and stir fry for another minute
  4. Add in tomatoes and pour soy sauce. Toss and stir fry for 2-3 min
  5. Season with salt and white pepper to taste
