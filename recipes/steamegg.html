Steamed Egg

Description

Delicate, silky smooth steamed egg that is so easy to make, so easy to gobble down!

Ingredients

Serving size: 4 people

  • 4 eggs (200ml)
  • 2 parts warm water (400ml)
  • 2 stalk scallions
  • 1/2 tbsp salt
  • 2 tsp light soy sauce
  • dash of white pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. Boil water in a pan with steamer rack then reduce to simmer.
  2. Crack the egg into a mixing bowl.
  3. Add salt & whisk.
  4. Use a strainer and pour the eggs in as you sieve away the bubbles.
  5. Cover and leave a gap for excessive steam to escape.
  6. Steam for about 5-10 min. Eggs should shake with a gelatin-like consistency.
  7. Switch off heat & remove steamed eggs from steamer.
  8. Add sauce, season with white pepper and garnish with scallions and serve.
